About me

I am a graphic designer form Hungary, living mostly in California. I have tried different crafts, but I was never so much in love with any art form as I am with felting now. I enjoy working with wool very much and I create each of my items with lots of love and care.

My creations are Waldorf inspired, but I am self taught and use wool and other inlayed materials in a unique way. I use colorful wool and mix them with natural found objects or recycled and vintage material.
